What's this site about?

This site is an attempt to fill in the gaps that I may have lost in translation while studying Computer Science: Language Technology in Norway.

The notes are in English, but some of the extra resources linked may be in Norwegian.

While the main goal is to revise NLP concepts, I'm also using this as a way of getting some hands-on practice with front-end development tools, and to have an excuse to make and post some silly drawings.

If you are reading this, I hope this site will be useful for you too!
